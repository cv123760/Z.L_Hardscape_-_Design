About Our Company

We're a team of dedicated professionals passionate about creating beautiful, enduring outdoor living spaces that bring our clients' dreams to life.

Our Story

Founded in 2010 by Zachary Lee, Z.L Hardscape & Design began as a small landscaping operation with a big dream: to create beautiful, functional outdoor environments for homeowners and businesses alike. Over the years, we've grown into a trusted name in the industry, known for our creativity, attention to detail, and unwavering dedication to customer satisfaction. We believe every project is a partnership, and we work closely with our clients to ensure their vision is realized with precision and care.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: to provide exceptional landscaping services that enhance the beauty and value of our clients' properties. We strive to create outdoor spaces that are not only visually stunning but also functional, sustainable, and perfectly tailored to our clients' unique needs and preferences. Quality craftsmanship and lasting relationships are the cornerstones of our business.
